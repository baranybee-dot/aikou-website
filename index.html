Featured Work
neoscan
AI-Powered Skin Lesion Analysis

We built Neoscan, an AI-powered tool that analyzes uploaded photos of skin lesions and birthmarks to estimate the likelihood of malignancy. Users get a clear probability score, helping them decide whether to seek medical attention before it's too late.

The model was trained on a large-scale dermatology dataset provided by Harvard University, bringing its classification accuracy close to state-of-the-art clinical solutions, making early skin cancer screening more accessible to everyone.

lumé
AI-Powered Beauty Coaching App

We built Lumé, an AI-powered facial analysis platform that delivers honest, science-backed beauty coaching. The app analyzes selfies across four key dimensions, harmony, structure, skin quality, and grooming, generating precision scores and a fully personalized improvement roadmap.

By combining computer vision with evidence-based coaching, Lumé goes beyond filters and subjective opinions. Users follow 30-day challenges, track weekly progress, and compare before/after results to achieve real, measurable improvements.

Computer Vision Facial Analysis AI Personalized Coaching

haigo
AI-Powered Recommendation Platform

We built Haigo, an AI-powered recommendation platform that helps users find venues perfectly matching their mood. The product combines Google Places data with advanced Generative AI, which interprets free-text requests and analyzes social chatter to deliver curated results.

With its blend of location intelligence and AI, the solution moves beyond ratings and filters to provide personalized, meaningful suggestions, making discovering new places faster, smarter, and more enjoyable.
